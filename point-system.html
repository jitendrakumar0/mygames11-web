. Substitutes other than Concussion substitutes, COVID-19, or X-Factor substitutes will not beawarded points for any contribution they make.

. If a player is announced in the starting eleven of a team but later is unable to start thematch, they will not score any points. However, the player who plays as the replacement willearn points for the match (including starting points).
. No points will be awarded for any actions during a Super Over or a Super Five.
. No further adjustments will be made to the points awarded once a match has beenmarked as completed and winners have been declared. Points awarded for a LIVE game will be subject to change only when the match status is “In Progress” or “In Review”.
. More than 11 players from either team can play in Other OD/Other T20/Other Test matches.® There are no negative points for low Strike Rates in Test matches.
